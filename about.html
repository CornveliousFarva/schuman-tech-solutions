    ABOUT ME

    My name is Greg Schuman, and I am an aspiring tech professional. Having gotten the bug for tech a few years ago, I have learned that it is a state of constant growth and challenges.

    I've always loved being a problem-solver, whether it was helping to put together an inventory management system from scratch, or developing a safety plan on the fly during a massive power outage. It isn't always easy and there are times it can get the best of me, but I have also learned to never give up.

    I also like to help people. Sometimes the smallest kind gesture can make the biggest impact.

    After taking time away from tech to focus on my family after a series of deaths and health scares in the past year, I'm re-focusing my goals towards professional development.

    The key to success is a healthy work-life balance. When I'm not working, I'm learning American Sign Language, playing my banjo or violin, or getting some time in on my rowing machine.
